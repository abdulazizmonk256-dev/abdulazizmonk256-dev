This page is a playable UI prototype (top-down training sim) that demonstrates the systems: puzzles → power charges → bullet effects, telekinesis push/pull, stealth visibility, parry timing, flight stamina, leveling + enemy HP scaling.

Core Systems (Design Spec)

Scaling
Leveling (Kills → XP → Stats)
Each kill grants XP. Level-ups increase move speed, damage, power strength, and flight stamina. Enemy HP scales with level + wave.
Stealth + Crouch
Visibility meter drops while crouched/stealthed; enemies acquire you slower. Firing unsuppressed weapons spikes visibility.
Melee + Parry Window
Parry (Q) briefly opens a timing window. Successful parry stuns the attacker and empowers next melee hit.
Telekinesis (Directional Push/Pull)
Hold T and drag to choose direction and force. Pull to you, push away, or throw sideways.

Training Sim (Top‑Down)
Move WASD. Aim with mouse; shoot LMB, aim RMB. Crouch C, stealth V, sprint Shift. Melee F, parry Q. Telekinesis: hold T + drag. Flight toggle X (uses stamina); ascend Space / descend Ctrl while flying.
